## BRAND VOICE REFERENCE (for all writers)

### English (EN) — bornworks tone
- Active voice, direct. No passive constructions like "Our solutions are delivered..."
- Address the reader as "you" when explaining value; use "we" for bornworks actions
- Confident without being arrogant: "We build..." not "We try to build..."
- Technical but accessible: define jargon when it first appears
- Founder-friendly: acknowledges the reader is busy and cares about ROI
- Example EN: "We ship early and iterate quickly. No over-engineering, no endless planning."

### Indonesian (ID) — natural, not translated
- Use "kami" for bornworks, "Anda" or "kamu" for reader
- Natural contractions: "udah", "bikin", "bisa", "gue" (semi-formal), "dong"
- Avoid calques: do NOT literally translate EN idioms into ID
- Active, direct sentences. Not "Kami menyediakan jasa yang bertujuan untuk..." — say "Kami bikin..."
- Short paragraphs. Conversational.
- Example ID: "Kami ship lebih awal dan iterasi cepat. Tanpa over-engineering atau perencanaan yang berlarut."

**Implementation note for engineer:** The hreflang for the EN version (`/path`) should NOT include a self-referential `hreflang="en"` pointing to itself — it should only have `hreflang="id"` pointing to the ID version and `hreflang="x-default"` pointing to the EN default.
